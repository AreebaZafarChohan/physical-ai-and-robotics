# Quiz: Capstone Project: Autonomous Humanoid Robot

Test your understanding of the challenges and integrated concepts in building an autonomous humanoid robot.

---

## Question 1

Which of the following is NOT a core capability expected of an autonomous humanoid robot?

a) Perceiving its environment and its own state.
b) Understanding high-level human commands.
c) Generating its own power supply.
d) Reasoning about tasks and generating plans.

**Answer**: c) Generating its own power supply.

---

## Question 2

In the context of an autonomous humanoid, what is the primary role of Visual SLAM (Vslam)?

a) To control the robot's grippers.
b) To simultaneously localize the robot and map its unknown environment using visual input.
c) To generate human-like speech.
d) To plan high-level cognitive tasks.

**Answer**: b) To simultaneously localize the robot and map its unknown environment using visual input.

---

## Question 3

How do Large Language Models (LLMs) contribute to cognitive planning in an autonomous humanoid?

a) They directly control the robot's joint movements.
b) They provide low-level sensor data filtering.
c) They interpret abstract human commands, decompose goals, and contribute to high-level reasoning.
d) They are primarily used for rendering realistic robot visuals in simulation.

**Answer**: c) They interpret abstract human commands, decompose goals, and contribute to high-level reasoning.

---

## Question 4

What is a significant challenge in humanoid robot locomotion compared to wheeled robots?

a) Simpler sensor integration.
b) Less computational power required.
c) Maintaining dynamic balance and complex multi-joint control on varied terrain.
d) Only operating in highly structured environments.

**Answer**: c) Maintaining dynamic balance and complex multi-joint control on varied terrain.

---

## Question 5

Which simulation platform is highlighted for its ability to accelerate Reinforcement Learning (RL) training for complex humanoid behaviors through GPU-accelerated physics and parallel simulations?

a) Gazebo
b) Unity
c) RViz
d) NVIDIA Isaac Sim

**Answer**: d) NVIDIA Isaac Sim
